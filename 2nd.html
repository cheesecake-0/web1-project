SOUTHERN ROUTE

Discover the old roman empire

A Mediterranean Journey Like No Other

The Southern Route is your gateway to the sun-drenched coastlines and rich cultures of Southern Europe and Northern Africa. This immersive journey takes you through some of the most historic and picturesque locations in the Mediterranean region, blending old-world charm with vibrant modern life. Perfect for those who crave a mix of history, cuisine, and seaside relaxation, this route is a cultural mosaic waiting to be explored.

What’s Included?

  • SPAIN

    – Wander through ancient cities, experience flamenco, and taste world-famous tapas in cities like Madrid, Seville, and Barcelona.

  • FRANCE

    – Enjoy the elegance of the French Riviera, the romance of Provence, and the culinary delights of the south.

  • ITALY

    – From the canals of Venice to the rolling hills of Tuscany and the coastline of Amalfi, Italy offers timeless beauty and rich traditions.

  • MAROCCO

    – Step into the exotic charm of Marrakech, Fes, and Casablanca. Explore vibrant souks, desert landscapes, and a fusion of Arab, Berber, and French influences.

  • MALTA

    – Indulge in luxury in this glamorous principality, known for its casinos, yachts, and the stunning Monte Carlo coastline.
