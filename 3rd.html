NORTHERN ROUTE

NORTHERN ROUTE

Feal like a viking

Explore the Wonders of the North

Our Northern Route invites you on a breathtaking journey through Northern Europe and Greenland — a region known for its dramatic landscapes, rich history, and unique cultural experiences. This route is perfect for travelers who love nature, cool climates, and stunning coastal and arctic scenery. From the fjords of Norway to the icy expanse of Greenland, this adventure is filled with unforgettable sights and experiences.

What’s Included?

  • NORWEY

    – Cruise through majestic fjords, visit Viking heritage sites, and explore charming cities like Bergen and Oslo.

  • UNITED KINGDOMS (UK)

    – Discover a blend of tradition and modern life in cities like London, Edinburgh, and Belfast.

  • IRELAND

    – Enjoy lush green landscapes, ancient castles, and the warm hospitality of Dublin and beyond.

  • ICELAND

    – Witness geysers, waterfalls, volcanoes, and the famous Blue Lagoon — a land of raw natural power.

  • GREENLAND

    – Experience the vast icy wilderness, towering glaciers, and the culture of one of the world’s most remote regions.
